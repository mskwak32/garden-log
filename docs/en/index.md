# Garden Diary Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 21, 2026**

Garden Diary (the "App") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and handles information.

---

## 1. Purpose of Data Processing

The App processes data for the following purposes:

- Analyzing app usage and improving features (Firebase Analytics)
- Providing advertisements and measuring their effectiveness (Google AdMob)

By using the App, you are deemed to have agreed to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Data Collected

The App does not collect information that directly identifies individual users.

The App does not collect personal information directly from users. However, some data may be automatically collected through third-party services.

| Service Provider | Data Collected | Collection Method |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Firebase Analytics (Google) | App usage events, device information (model, OS version), app version, approximate location (country/region), session information | Automatically collected during app usage |
| Firebase Crashlytics (Google) | Crash logs, device info, UUID | Automatically collected during crashes |
| Google AdMob (Google) | Advertising ID (GAID), device information, app usage patterns, ad interaction data | Automatically collected during ad display |

---

## 3. Data Retention

The App does not directly store personal data.

User-generated content (plant photos, diary logs) is stored locally on the device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Data collected via Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob is managed according to the data retention policies of Google LLC.

- Firebase Analytics: Subject to Google LLC policies
- Google AdMob: Subject to Google LLC policies

---

## 4. Data Deletion

The App does not directly store or manage personal data. Therefore, the developer cannot directly identify or delete data associated with a specific user. For data collected through Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google AdMob, users may request deletion directly through Google LLC.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services and International Data Transfer

The App does not directly provide collected data to third parties.

However, the following services process data independently:

| Service | Provider | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Firebase Analytics | Google LLC | Analytics and service improvement | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Firebase Crashlytics | Google LLC | Crash reporting and stability improvement | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Google AdMob | Google LLC | Advertising and marketing | https://policies.google.com/privacy |

**International Data Transfer**

Data collected through these services may be stored and processed on servers located outside your country, including in the United States.

---

## 6. Automatic Data Collection and Opt-out

The App uses SDKs to automatically collect certain data for analytics and advertising purposes.

- Purpose: Usage analysis, error improvement, personalized advertising

**Opt-out (Advertising ID)**

Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset or delete advertising ID

Limiting ad tracking may affect personalized ads, but will not impact core app functionality.

Data collected via Firebase Analytics is processed in an aggregated and non-identifiable manner, and some data collection may be limited through device settings.

---

## 7. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of their data by contacting us via email at the address listed below.

However, since the App does not store identifiable personal data, the developer is technically unable to identify or retrieve data linked to a specific user. In such cases, we will inform you of this limitation and direct you to the appropriate third-party service.

For data collected via Google services, users should contact Google LLC directly.

**California Residents (CCPA)**

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of the sale of personal information.

However, since the App does not collect or store any information that can be linked to a specific individual, the developer is technically unable to identify, retrieve, or delete data associated with a particular user. As a result, requests for access or deletion cannot be fulfilled directly by the developer.

For data collected through Google services (Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Google AdMob), please contact Google LLC directly at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

- **Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing**: The App does not sell personal information. To limit interest-based advertising, reset or delete your Advertising ID as described in Section 6.
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: You will not be discriminated against for exercising your CCPA rights.
